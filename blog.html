How to Play

Mathegame is an interactive math-based game designed to make learning fun and engaging. Here's how it works:

  1. Start the game by clicking the "Play" button on the main menu.
  2. You will be presented with a series of math challenges, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division problems.
  3. Answer each question within the time limit to earn points.
  4. If you answer correctly, you gain points and move to the next level. If you answer incorrectly, you lose a life.
  5. The game ends when you run out of lives or complete all levels.

Features

  • Multiple difficulty levels to suit players of all ages.
  • Timed challenges to test your speed and accuracy.
  • Leaderboard to track your high scores and compete with friends.
